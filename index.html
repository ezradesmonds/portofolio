01. About Me

Engineering with Empathy

I am a student at Petra Christian University, deeply immersed in the world of software development and Artificial Intelligence. My journey started with a curiosity for how things work, leading me to explore everything from frontend architectures with Next.js to backend logic and deep learning models.

What sets my approach apart is a strong focus on UX design principles. I firmly believe that powerful code is useless if it's not accessible. I rigorously apply Jakob Nielsen’s 10 heuristics to ensure the interfaces I build are as logical and robust as the algorithms running behind them.

Beyond writing code, I value leadership and teamwork. As the Head of the IT Division for the Innofashion Show 8, I led and mentored a team to deliver a seamless event platform. My background in OSIS as a treasurer and event chairman has also equipped me with strong project management and financial acumen.

When I'm not debugging or analyzing network packets with Wireshark, you'll probably find me analyzing financial markets, producing music (proudly a certified Best Keyboardist!), or unwinding with a match of Brawlhalla.
